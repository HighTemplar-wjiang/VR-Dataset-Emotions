Inducing Emotions in Virtual Reality

VR 60 mins Install app

Dive into a unique experience where virtual reality meets emotions. In this study, we invite you to immerse yourself in various VR scenes crafted to evoke a spectrum of emotions. The entire process will take up to 60 minutes.

Requirements: You should own or have access to a Meta Quest 2 VR device.

Here's what you'll be doing:

  • Complete a brief pre-study survey (~5 min).
  • Install our VR app using SideQuest (Experienced: ~5 min | Beginners: ~20 min).
  • Engage with the VR app & complete the survey (~15 min).
  • Finish a post-study survey (~10 min).
